Pokédex Help

The Pokédex feature is here to help you search through Pokémon and be able to see more about them. You can use filters to search specific types or generations.

Team Building

Team building can take lots of time and work. The best way to work on building a team is to think of a concept and search through Pokémon that can help fulfill that goal.

Badges

Badges are unlocked through making different kinds of teams. Each badge has different requirements. Some will be based on types, weaknesses, or super effectiveness.
